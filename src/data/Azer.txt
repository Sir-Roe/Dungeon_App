An Azer is a dwarven creature from the elemental plane of fire. They appear as stocky, muscular dwarves with fiery beards and hair, and their skin is made of living metal. They are expert craftsmen and are often employed as blacksmiths or metalworkers. Their bodies radiate intense heat, making them resistant to cold-based attacks but vulnerable to fire-based attacks. They have a fierce loyalty to their allies and will do whatever it takes to protect them, making them valuable allies in battle.
