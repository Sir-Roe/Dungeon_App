The adult copper dragon is a metallic dragon known for their love of humor and trickery. Their scales are a pale, metallic copper color and they have a long, sinewy body with wings and sharp claws. Their powerful breath weapon is a cone of acid, which can quickly dissolve anything in its path. They are wise and intelligent creatures, often acting as mentors or advisors to those seeking their guidance. Despite their mischievous nature, they are fiercely protective of their allies and will fight to the death to defend them.
