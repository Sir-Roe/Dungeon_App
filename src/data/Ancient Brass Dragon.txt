An Ancient Brass Dragon is a legendary creature from the Dungeons and Dragons fantasy game. These powerful dragons are known for their expert ability to burrow and create underground lairs. They also possess a breath weapon of deadly, scorching flames and are highly skilled in magic. Ancient Brass Dragons are wise and cunning, and they live for centuries, amassing vast hordes of treasure and knowledge. They are formidable foes and powerful allies, revered for their mastery of fire and earth magic.
